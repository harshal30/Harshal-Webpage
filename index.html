Harshal Chaudhari received his B.E. degree in Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering from Pimpri Chinchwad College of Engineering, Pune University, Maharashtra, India, in 2018. He completed his M.Tech degree in Telecommunication at the National Institute of Technology Calicut, Kerala, India, in 2020. Currently, he is a research scholar in the Department of Electronics and Electrical Engineering (EEE) at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Guwahati, Assam, India. His research focuses on digital holographic microscopy, quantitative phase imaging, biomedical imaging, and deep learning.
